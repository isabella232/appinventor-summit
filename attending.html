TRAVEL TO CAMBRIDGE & MIT

Cambridge is in the Boston metropolitan area, served by Boston Logan International Airport.

For public transportation from the airport, take the MBTA (SL1) Silver Line (stops at every terminal) to South Station. At South Station take the Red Line towards Alewife and stop at Kendall/MIT. The cost is $2.00. The travel time is approximately 30 minutes

A taxi from the Logan Airport to Kendall/MIT takes about 15 minutes and costs about $30.
