SUMMIT SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

Each summer, the MIT App Inventor Summit brings together 250 of the vast number of MIT App Inventor to learn, share and collaborate. Participants from all over the word share experiences around developing, refining, and promoting mobile apps using MIT App Inventor. These attendees then return to their communities to advocate for innovation in CS Education, promote the democratization of mobile app development and ultimately become local MIT App Inventor evangelists. As the flagship MIT App Inventor annual convening, all sessions are professionally video recorded and shared with the more than 1.5 million member global App Inventor community providing event sponsors with long-term brand exposure.
